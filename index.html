About UAgenda

Hello! If you have come to this website, you are probably a student in search of a way to stop procrastinating and organize your life.

You may have tried different time-management or scheduling calendar techniques such as using a physical agenda, Google Calendar or your university provided scheduling tool. However, none of these services are optimized to provide all the functionalities that are needed to stay organized.

With UAgenda, we provide an individualized course scheduling service that optimizes your day-to-day schedule, so you won’t ever have to worry about planning what to do next.

UAgenda Plans

The Basic plan fetches your course schedule and creates a personalized agenda for you.

If you decide to upgrade to the Premium plan, you will benefit from:

  • Cutting-edge artificial intelligence that will provide time management suggestions based on what you would like to do in your free time.
  • Time-management suggestions for different activities such as studying, relaxation and exercising that can be added into your schedule.
  • Scheduled course time will provide links to your virtual campus page.
  • Notifications and reminders regarding upcoming deadlines, courses and time-management suggestions

Today’s world requires to juggle many online platforms and it is crucial to have reliable ways to accurately manage your time. UAgenda will change scheduling from a boring task to a fun interactive activity.
